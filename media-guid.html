BEFORE THE EVENT

  1. Please register yourself by filling up the media registration form
  2. Review the programme of the event to find which of activities you would like to focus on.
  3. Stay tuned and carefully observe the updated published by the organiser
  4. Make sure to attend the briefing for media which will take place a few days before the event
  5. For international media representatives, please contact the organiser if you need a letter to facilitate getting your visa to Malaysia
  6. Try to collect the media badge prior to the first day of the event

DURING THE EVENT

  1. Display your media badge everywhere you go within the halls of the event
  2. Please adhere to the rules set by the organiser regarding media presence in various functions
  3. Make sure to make use of the facilities provided in the media room, details of which will be provided in your media kit
  4. If you require any assistance to conduct interviews or meet some of the special guests please contact the organiser to get the proper arrangement
  5. All the information about the exhibitors and speakers will be provided in the event directory which will be provided to all media representatives

AFTER THE EVENT

  1. Make sure to fill up the media review form
  2. You may contact the organiser to get additional materials such as photos or videos to help featuring your story about the event
  3. Observe the updates of the following event by OIC International Business Centre and be part of them
