WHO SHOULD EXHIBIT?

Looking at the comprehensive nature of the exhibition zone at the Muslim World Biz, the range of participation is wide. In particular, companies working in banking and finance, from food & beverage pharmaceuticals & cosmetics, higher education, e-commerce and logistics, healthcare and tourism are invited to book their exhibition space at the respective section.

Exhibitors will not only be able to showcase products and services to local and international buyers, but they will also be part of the business matching session which will be orgnaised concurrently along the days of the exhibition. The worth of the business transactions increases exponentially every year with more participants joining the event both as exhibitors and trade buyers.

WHY EXHIBIT?

  1. Networking opportunities
  2. Business Matching Prospect
  3. Business Partnership
  4. Investment Opportunities
  5. Access to the entire Muslim World market, more than 50 countries.
  6. Brand/ Product Awareness
  7. Media Exposure
